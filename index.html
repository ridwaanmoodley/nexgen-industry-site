HOW IT WORKS

NEXGEN HOLOGRAM

Your Brand Floating in Mid-Air

The hologram unit's LED blades spin at high speed while flashing in sequence to create a floating 3D image — no screen, no glass, nothing between your content and your customer. Upload via WiFi or TF card and update your content anytime. Plug in and it runs. Simple as that.

TAPLINK

One Tap Shares Everything

Hold any NFC-enabled smartphone near a TapLink card or table talker. A digital profile page instantly pops up with your contact info, social media, WhatsApp, Google Reviews link, website and more. The person hits save and your details are in their phone permanently. No app. No QR code to hunt for. Just tap.
